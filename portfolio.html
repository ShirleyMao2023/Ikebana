Ikebana Arrangements
Portfolio Reflection

Symphony

The theme of my final arrangement is harmony. All the plant materials in the arrangement and the vase paint work in harmony, just like the various instruments in a symphony. Additionally, the plants in the arrangement appear to be swaying to some music of nature, we can only imagine.

Waiting for Spring

The theme of this arrangement is waiting for the true arrival of spring. While waiting for winter to pass, we can appreciate a fragment of spring from the arrangement. The angel roots mimic bare branches and contrast the dainty flowers to accentuate the liveliness of the plants in the vase.

Autumn

This arrangement represents the changing of seasons from summer to autumn by using the varying colors in the flowers from yellow-orange to red-orange. Orange and yellow colors of the flowers are also very warm and evoke lots of happy emotions.

Sway with the Wind


The essence of this slanted arrangement is motion. The key point of the composition is the wavy spirea branches. The curvature and orientation of these branches in relation to the dark red carnation appears as if the flowerâ€™s arms are swaying in synchronization with the wind.

Roller Coaster

This arrangement conveys the sense of exhilarating and weightless excitement. I used the weeping willow to create a slanted structure for the composition. Near the opening of the vase, we have the gerbera daisy as our centerpiece, highlighting the climax of the exciting journey.

Happy Explosions

The concept of this arrangement is uncontainable happiness. This is a vertical arrangement with a focus on points. The abundance of points creates movement and action akin to a firework display of happiness, where there is a big burst of dazzling light and then a chain reaction of smaller bursts.

A Summer Afternoon

The theme of the arrangement is the uplifting yet content peaceful happiness, analogous to enjoying the warmth of sunshine while taking a stroll in the park. This vertical composition has the likeness of a sunflower stretching its leafy arms to the sky to shower in the warmth of sunshine.

Beautiful Moment in Life

Here we have a vertical arrangement. Following the path of the carnations to milkweed, we see the cycle of birth, growth, and withering away represented by the green bud, blooming carnations, and milkweed respectively. The harmony of the elements in this composition conveys the beauty in all stages of life.
